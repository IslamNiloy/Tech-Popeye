Recruitment Process

Understanding Your Needs

To begin, the employer and the KBG Recruiting Agency should sign an agency agreement outlining the terms and conditions of the demand and supply of manpower from Bangladesh. This agreement should be signed by both parties in order for the stated terms and conditions to be accepted and the demand to be executed.

we study the client's requirements and consult with their representative to gain a thorough understanding of their requirements. we collaborate closely with our clients to fully understand their needs so that we canexpand our business relationship. Then we ask the clients to submit the required documents, which are discussedfurther below under the heading Employer's part

Employer's Part

Power of Attorney

The employer/principal will grant a power of attorney to the KBG Recruiting Agency as a true and lawfulattorney and agent in Bangladesh for handling all recruitment affairs with the Bangladesh Government on behalf of the employer/principal

Demand Letter

The employer/principal will formally issue a demand letter in favour of KBG Recruiting Agency outlining the job categories, number of workers required in each category, monthly salary, duty hours, contract duration, and other worker amenities such as food, medical, transportation, air passage, accommodation, and other benefits.

Visa Documents

Please keep in mind that this document requirement is only applicable to the Kingdom of Saudi Arabia. The employer/principal will provide visa documents okala that will allow the Government of the employing country to hire such workers from Bangladesh. It must be original and authenticated in order to be accepted by the respective embassy

Company Registration (CR) Copy

Again please note that, this particular requirement of document is only applicable for Kingdom of Saudi Arabia. A letter addressing the Consular General of the recruiting country to allow recruitment of candidates from Bangladesh as per demand letter against the stated visa block number.

Employment Agreement

Copies of standard employment contract between employer and employee are required.

Agency Agreement

The employer and KBG Recruiting Agency should sign an agency agreement with all the terms and conditions which will be the guide for both the parties. Authorization Letter, P/A and Demand Letter should be in original, duly sealed & signed by the sponsor or authorized person & attested by Chamber of Commerce and Ministry of Foreign Affairs. Upon receipt of all necessary documents mentioned above, KBG Recruiting Agency will be able to deploy the recruited persons to the destination of the employer within 14-45 days' time subject to availability of seats in airlines concerned
