Mr. Mohammed Enamul Kabir Khan

Chairman

Mohammed Enamul Kabir Khan the Chairman of Khan Brothers PP woven Bag Industries Ltd. was born in December 30, 1965 in Kishoreganj, Bangladesh. He completed his Honors and Masters in Accounting from the University of Dhaka in 1990 & 1991 respectively and he started his professional career with city bank limited He served with Aljajira, Jeddah and Saudi Arabia bank for 18 years as a manager, has vast experience with foreign exchange and maintaining key accounts. He was gathered diversified experience to be able to serve in different managerial position in multiple companies. During his tenure in different corporate experiences in both locally and abroad, he gathered and learned the values of business which encourage him to be an entrepreneur of Khan Brothers PP woven Bag Industries Limited and allied concerns.

Mohammad Ruhul Kabir Khan

Vice-Chairman

Mohammad Ruhul Kabir Khan is leading Khan Brothers Group as a director for more than 19 years now. He has held the position with utmost professionalism and skill. He has completed M.Com in Management from University of Dhaka. He began his professional career with City Bank Limited and later Joined Biman Bangladesh, the national airlines of Bangladesh as a Manager. He used his years of management and administrative experiences for the constant betterment of Khan Brothers Group and its allied concerns. The company has achieved many administrative milestones because of his valuable contribution.
